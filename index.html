Project 2

Factory Management Application This application is an ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) solution developed specifically for factory businesses, and is designed to work on mobile platforms using Flutter and Dart technologies. Additionally, MySQL is used as the database, thus providing strong data management. Key Features: Category and Product Management: Categorizing and managing the products produced or stocked by the factory. Users can add it to their favorites and add it to their cart. Order Management: Users can create orders, make payments and track their order status through the application. Payment transactions are carried out securely. Mobile and Web Compatible: It offers seamless operation on both mobile application and web browser. In this way, managers and employees can access anytime and anywhere. This ERP solution is designed to increase the efficiency of businesses and improve decision-making processes, with a focus on optimizing factory management processes. With its user-friendly interface and reliable structure, it helps factories manage their daily operations effectively.
